P2P Delivers Peace Of Mind

Corporate event managers know that big corporate events and tradeshows are more complex and unique than what UPS/FedEx can handle.

Complexities may include: Corporate venue delivery specifics, odd size items to ship, temporary warehousing, and shipping troubleshooting by people who actually understand what should happen.

“The best part of my job is making genuine connections with clients. It’s energizing to see projects unfold, and working with our engaged internal team is fun as we tackle challenges while ensuring successful deliveries. It’s like being part of a backstage crew for a theater, we are part of the behind the scenes team that contribute to a great show.”

P2P Handles The Chaos & Distractions

As an extension of large company agencies and internal event teams, P2P handles ALL the chaos and distractions of getting stuff there.

As a result, companies can focus on delivering a great event experience.

Being the national leader and a trusted partner, 90% of P2P’s projects involve owning the custom shipping solutions for large corporate events (Microsoft Ignite / PSX / DOTA).

The other 10% of projects are helping companies outsource shipping and event logistics while attending a large conference or trade show (CES / E3).
